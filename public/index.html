Get to know Joseph


Joseph Aiken is a junior web developer living in Charlotte, NC seeking creative opportunities in Front End Development


A graduate of UNC Charlotte's Full Stack program, and currently employed as a Front End Developer, Joseph has gained proficient skills in HTML, CSS, SCSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Gulp, and Git.


In his spare time Joseph enjoys playing guitar, and hiking with his fiance, Kathleen, and their dog, Piper. Joseph can also be found attending shows in the lively Charlotte music scene.
